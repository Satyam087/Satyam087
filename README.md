## The "Predict → Decide → Act" Philosophy

I believe the next era of computing is **Agentic**, not conversational. Every system I build follows one mental model:

| Phase | What It Means | How I Build It |
|-------|--------------|----------------|
| **Predict** | Understand the future from data | Classical ML, regressions, probabilistic models |
| **Decide** | Choose the best path deterministically | Multi-agent consensus, business logic, orchestration |
| **Act** | Interact with the world | LLMs as the execution and communication layer |

My goal: move beyond "System 1" (instinctive/probabilistic) AI and build "System 2" (logical/analytical) architectures that companies can trust with their bottom line.
